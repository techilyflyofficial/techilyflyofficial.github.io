Return and Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Policy Overview

At Techily Fly, we are committed to delivering high-quality digital services that meet and exceed our clients' expectations. This Return and Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be issued for our digital services.

Please note that digital services, including but not limited to website development, video production, digital marketing, and AI automation services, are subject to specific refund considerations due to their intangible nature and the intellectual property involved.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered under the following circumstances:

Before Work Commencement

If a client cancels a project before any work has begun:

  • Full refund (100%) may be provided if cancellation occurs within 24 hours of payment
  • 80% refund if cancellation occurs between 24 hours and 7 days of payment
  • 50% refund if cancellation occurs between 7 days and 14 days of payment
  • No refund for cancellations after 14 days from payment date

After Work Commencement

Once work has commenced on your project:

  • No refunds for the portion of work that has been completed
  • Partial refund may be considered for uncompleted portions of the project
  • Refund amount will be calculated based on work completed vs. total project scope
  • Admin fees (20% of total project cost) may apply to all post-commencement refunds

Quality Issues

If you believe there are quality issues with delivered work:

  • We will first attempt to address concerns through revisions
  • Refunds may be considered if work fundamentally doesn't meet agreed specifications
  • All quality concerns must be documented in writing within 7 days of delivery
  • Techily Fly reserves the right to demonstrate that deliverables meet agreed standards

Non-Refundable Services

The following are generally non-refundable:

  • Consultation fees and strategy sessions
  • Domain names and hosting services (subject to provider policies)
  • Third-party software licenses or subscriptions
  • Completed work that meets agreed specifications
  • Services rendered beyond the agreed revision limits
  • Additional work requested outside the original scope
  • Services delayed due to client-provided content or feedback delays

Refund Request Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Contact Us

Send a written refund request to TechilyFly@gmail.com with the following information:

  • Your full name and contact information
  • Project name or invoice number
  • Reason for refund request
  • Detailed explanation of the issue
  • Any supporting documentation

Step 2: Review Period

Once we receive your request:

  • We will acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
  • Our team will review your request within 7 business days
  • We may request additional information or clarification
  • We may offer alternative solutions before processing a refund

Step 3: Decision and Processing

After review:

  • You will receive our decision in writing
  • If approved, refunds will be processed within 14 business days
  • Refunds will be issued using the original payment method
  • Payment processor fees may be deducted from refund amounts

Service Modifications and Credits

Instead of cash refunds, we may offer:

  • Credit for future services (valid for 12 months)
  • Service modifications or alternatives
  • Additional revisions or improvements
  • Upgraded service packages

Project Milestones and Partial Deliverables

For projects with multiple milestones:

  • Refunds are calculated per completed milestone
  • Each milestone is considered a separate deliverable
  • Completed milestones are non-refundable unless quality issues are proven
  • Uncompleted milestones may be eligible for partial refund

Force Majeure and External Factors

Techily Fly is not liable for refunds in cases of:

  • Client bankruptcy or insolvency
  • Changes in client business circumstances
  • Third-party service failures beyond our control
  • Acts of nature, war, or government actions
  • Industry-wide technical issues or outages

Digital Product Refunds

For digital products (templates, presets, etc.):

  • 7-day refund window from purchase date
  • Products must be unused and unmodified
  • Digital downloads are non-refundable once accessed
  • Technical issues will be resolved before considering refunds

Subscription Services

For recurring subscription services:

  • Cancellation takes effect at the next billing cycle
  • Partial refunds are not provided for unused portions
  • Service access continues until the end of the paid period
  • Downgrades may be processed with prorated adjustments

Client Cooperation Requirements

Refund eligibility requires:

  • Timely provision of required materials and feedback
  • Adherence to agreed communication channels and timelines
  • Reasonable response times (within 48-72 hours)
  • Professional conduct throughout the project

Projects significantly delayed due to client non-cooperation may not be eligible for refunds.

Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with our refund decision:

  • Provide additional evidence or documentation
  • Request a review by a senior manager
  • Consider mediation through a neutral third party
  • Final decisions rest with Techily Fly management

Policy Changes

Techily Fly reserves the right to modify this Return and Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. The policy applicable to your purchase will be the one in effect at the time of your transaction.
